Latitude vs. Humidity


There does not seem to be a strong correlation between latitude and humidity. However, it is worth noting that there seem to be more locations with zero humidity in the northern hemisphere than in the southern hemisphere. There are also a surprising number of locations with 100% humidity scattered across almost all latitudes.
